<h1 class="w3-center">THE TREKS</h1>
<p class="w3-center w3-large"><b>"The journey of a thousand miles begins with a single step."</bs></p>
<div class="w3-row-padding " style="margin-top:64px">
<div class="w3-col l3 m6 w3-margin-bottom">
<div class="w3-card">
<img src="https://cdn.s3waas.gov.in/s345fbc6d3e05ebd93369ce542e8f2322d/uploads/bfi_thumb/2018030613-olw8slkklk0je8pq0rpiaie279yu3l1g3t2vz9uaoa.jpg" style="width:100%">
<div class="w3-container">
<h3><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bhimashankar_Temple">BHIMASHANKR TREK</a></h3>
<p class="w3-opacity">Nature lover's delight </p>
<p>The trail to Bhimashankar is an all-time favourite amongst trekkers and more so during the monsoons. It takes you right into the heart of Bhimashankar Wildlife sanctuary. While in the jungle, you will have a variety of birds, langurs and spotted dear for company. If you are lucky, you might even get to spot a "Shekru". The best part of the trek is the trail to Gupt Bhimashankar.
</p>
</div>
</div>
</div>
<div class="w3-col l3 m6 w3-margin-bottom">
<div class="w3-card">
<img src="https://bragpacker.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/10/related-image-1-1024x630.jpeg" style="width:100%">
<div class="w3-container">
<h3> <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kalsubai">KALSU BAI</a></h3>
<p class="w3-opacity">The Highest Peack Of Maharashtra</p>
<p>The Kalsubai Trek is considered to be the highest peak and is the premier of all the treks in the state of Maharashtra. It is located at 5400 ft, and is moderately difficult.Kalsubai temple is established at the zenith, and every trekker is drawn to this small shrine, sublimely surrounded with the regally scenic view. It is known as the Everest of Maharashtra.
</p>
</div>
</div>
</div>
<div class="w3-col l3 m6 w3-margin-bottom">
<div class="w3-card">
<img src="https://www.onacheaptrip.com/wp-content/uploads/River-at-Devkund-Trek.jpeg" style="width:100%">
<div class="w3-container">
<h3><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Devkund_Waterfall">DEVKUND WATERFALL TREK</a></h3>
<p class="w3-opacity">Beautiful amalgamation of three waterfalls </p>
<p>Devkund waterfall trek is a tiny, shielded and fortified place near Bhira dam. It is located at an altitude of 2000 feet. Devkund is a union of three waterfalls and it is where the Kundalika river originates. The local residents consider it pious and reverent, and likes to believe that it is the "The bathing ponds of Gods."
</p>
</div>
</div>
</div>
<div class="w3-col l3 m6 w3-margin-bottom">
<div class="w3-card">
<img src="https://encrypted-tbn0.gstatic.com/images?q=tbn:ANd9GcQvQvBZDbzKLCByUaKdubbZDuSa754PwKKURg&usqp=CAU" style="width:100%">
<div class="w3-container">
<h3><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kalavantin_Durg">KALAVANTIN DURG</a></h3>
<p class="w3-opacity">Climb To Heaven</p>
<p>It is one of the most perilous and terrifying treks, and sited at around 2,205 feet in the Rajgad district. The apex of the Kalavantin is an nerve-wracking and alarming experience and is the best place for travel junkies.The durg was built in the prestige and honor of the queen Kalavati, also illustrated as "Climb to Heaven"</p>
